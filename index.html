Remember when your Mom used to tell you not to use your computer in the dark?


Well, she was right.

Your eyes are designed to look at light reflecting off objects, not the light source itself. By lighting the wall behind your display an appopriate color your eyes are able to widen their focus to include the wall visible around your display. This creates the experience of viewing content on a larger display which is more natural for your eyes. Blah filler content that all needs to be re-written...
